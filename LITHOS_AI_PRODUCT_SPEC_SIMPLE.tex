\section*{Executive Summary}

Lithos AI is a Bloomberg Terminal-inspired platform supercharged by artificial intelligence for the critical minerals industry. The platform transforms months of mining diligence into minutes by ingesting and analyzing drill-hole logs, technical reports, satellite imagery, offtake contracts, and ESG filings through fine-tuned LLMs and geospatial models.

The platform delivers instant diligence notes, cash-flow forecasts, and supply-chain risk maps for miners, traders, and financiers, replacing fragmented workflows with an integrated solution that accelerates decision-making in the \$7.1B resource intelligence market.

    \item \textbf{Document Processor:} Extracts structured data from PDFs and reports
    \item \textbf{News Aggregator:} Real-time monitoring of 500+ sources
    \item \textbf{Quality Assurance:} Validation and anomaly detection
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Processing Pipeline:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Source monitoring and change detection
    \item Document retrieval and parsing
    \item Data extraction and normalization
    \item Quality validation and error correction
    \item Database update and version control
    \item Alert generation and distribution
\end{enumerate}
